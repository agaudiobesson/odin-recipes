Guacamole Chicken Salad

Description

Best served al fresco on a hot summer's eve.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups shredded rotisserie chicken
  • 1 cup store-bought refrigerated guacamole

Steps

  1. Combine the chicken, guacamole, mango, onion, pepitas, lime juice and pesto in a mixing bowl, then season to taste with salt and pepper. Serve with lettuce leaves or flatbread for easy eating.
